## Core positioning
I do **not** want to market myself primarily as a pure software engineer.

That would undersell the strongest parts of my profile and would not feel fully honest.

At the same time, I **do** want to show my software development side clearly. I have real development experience, I can contribute technically, and I have been involved in real implementation work.

### Best positioning
**AI, automation, and technology consultant with strong technical grounding**

Alternative framing:
- **AI and automation-focused technical problem solver**
- **Product-minded technical builder focused on AI and practical workflows**
- **Technology consultant with experience in AI enablement and software development**

### Desired balance
**60 / 40 split**
- **60%** AI, automation, consulting, product thinking, user-facing technology work, communication, enablement
- **40%** software development, implementation, technical depth, codebase familiarity, systems thinking

### Words / themes that fit me
- AI
- automation
- technology consulting
- practical problem solving
- user-centered technology
- product-minded
- cross-functional
- people-focused
- technically grounded
- thoughtful builder

### Words / themes to avoid overusing
- full-stack engineer
- software engineer first
- hardcore developer
- ML researcher
- startup founder energy
- generic “passionate about technology” language
